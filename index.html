Arethos was founded in 2005 by a team of passionate IT professionals with the vision to provide high quality, affordable software services to Singapore.

Arethos is a Microsoft's Certified Partner in Collaboration and Content Competency "stand out as a partner that is capable of delivering SharePoint solutions" since 2009.
